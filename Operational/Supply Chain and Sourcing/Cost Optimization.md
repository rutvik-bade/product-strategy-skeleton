Procurement efficiency and negotiation

# Quantitative levers

- Spend Pareto by supplier, category, SKU and plant.
- Purchase price variance against standard cost or last negotiated price.
- Total landed cost including freight, duty, warehousing, damage and payment terms.
- MOQ impact on [[Inventory]] carrying cost and stock obsolescence.
- Supplier cost-down pipeline with quantified savings, risk and implementation date.
