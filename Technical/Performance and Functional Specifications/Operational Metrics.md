Product-level operating metrics that define whether the product performs acceptably in the real world

# Metrics

- Uptime or availability
- Latency or response time
- Error rate
- Throughput
- Battery life or energy consumption
- MTBF
- MTTR
- Defect rate
- Environmental operating range
- Service-level threshold by customer segment or use case

# Decision tools

- SLA target setting
- Reliability growth tracking
- Pareto failure analysis
- Control charts
- Regression thresholds for release readiness
